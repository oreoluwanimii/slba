StrictlyLawBusinessAcademy

SLB Academy is created in recognition of the need for lawyers to understand and master the business side of law and leverage on same for efficient service delivery, sustainable growth and profitability in Legal Practice. Specifically, the establishment of the academy is informed by the aspiration to train and equip lawyers with knowledge and skills in the areas of law practice management, business development and enterprise. SLB Academy courses are designed to help lawyers for law practice management, legal department management and organization and business in general.

Vision

Our vision is to be the premier leading business school for lawyers.

Mission Statement

Our primary goal is to build a community of profitable lawyers. We are on a mission to help Lawyers understand and master the business side of law, develop strategies for monetizing legal skills and knowledge and ultimately make legitimate wealth from legal practice.

Statement Of Objective

At SLBA, our goal is to equip lawyers with the requisite business and management skills needed for a successful administration of their law firms. Through our influence and courses, participants see the need and potentials in transitioning from a solo practice firm to bigger firms/partnerships of well respected lawyers within the society.

ADMISSION

Are you interested in mastering the business side of law? Do you want to build wealth from law practice? Do you want to learn how to monetize your law practice? SLBA is the right place for you.
